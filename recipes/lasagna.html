Lasagna Recipe

Home Page

Description


Our favourite dishes and how to cook them!

Classic Italian dish prepared with tomato and beshamel sause!


Ingredients


  • Tomato sause
  • Bechamel sause
  • Bread covers
  • Cheese

Steps


  1. Create The Tomato and Bechamel sause
  2. Build your lasagna with bread cover then tomato sause, bread cover and the other sause (make at least 5 layers)
  3. After finishing the layers put cheese on the top
  4. Put in the oven for 40 minutes
  5. ENJOY!
